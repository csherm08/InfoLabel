The Info Label Mission

Info Label Inc. is a label printing and converting solutions company dedicated to exceptional quality, service, environmental sustainability, value, and excellence. By innovating, partnering, educating employees, and continuing to improve, Info Label will always stay focused on providing the very best package decoration solutions to our customers.

Meet the team!

Info Label prides itself on a family oriented team structure

Mark Dufort

Mark Dufort is the president and founder of Info Label, Inc. Mark began his career in the label converting business over 35 years ago as a Sales Representative for a New York label converter and worked his way up to General Manager. In 1994 Mark founded Info Label with the desire to serve specific markets with high quality, cost sensitive labels and a strong unwavering commitment to service. Mark is married and has four children. He is an avid basketball player, coach and fan, and enjoys a pickup game whenever he has the opportunity. Mark graduated from Siena College with a BA in Business.

Rosemary Joralemon

Rosemary Joralemon joined Info Label, Inc. in 2008 as Director of Sales and Marketing. She brings 25 years of award winning Sales, Sales Management and Marketing experience from such companies as Johnson & Johnson, Roche, and Quintiles. Rosemary is married and has three sons, three grandchildren, and two Labrador retrievers. She enjoys traveling, singing, and winter sports. Rosemary graduated from Fairfield University with a BA in Sociology.

Brad Bosco

In February of 2005, Bradley Bosco joined the Info Label Art Department. Brad previously worked with Apple Computer Inc. and as a Graphic Designer with Zone 5 Graphics. He has 17 years experience with Macintosh based computers and Adobe applications. Brad is married and has two children and a variety of pets. He enjoys sports and plays in a summer hockey league. The Info Label Plant

Info Label Inc. is located in our newly constructed 25, 000 square foot plant in Clifton Park, NY. Our equipment offering consist of four prime label presses housed in our 12,000 square foot of manufacturing space, and we store our wide variety of substrate material choices in our 5,000 square feet of warehouse space. We have over 3000 square feet of inventoried material options to assist in expediting customer orders.
